Our Philosophy

The OMMA Code

At OMMA, we believe the most powerful battle you will ever fight is the one within yourself. Our name is our philosophy.

O
O — First Principle
Ownership

True strength begins with ownership — of your body, your choices, your emotions, and your purpose. We train you to take full possession of your performance, your mindset, and your life.

M
M — Second Principle
Mindset

We adopt a disciplined, values-driven approach to mental conditioning. Drawing on principles of patience, resilience, self-mastery, and integrity, OMMA's mindset framework transforms how you handle adversity.

M
M — Third Principle
Martial

We embrace martial excellence in its truest sense — intelligent, non-aggressive control. The ability to neutralize threats with calm precision, affirm your strength without ego, and protect without harm.

A
A — Fourth Principle
Art

Martial arts is a craft refined over centuries. At OMMA, we honor the traditions of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and MMA while evolving the practice into something deeply personal — a language you speak through movement.

Coach
Mohammed Doty

Some coaches teach technique. Coach Mohammed Doty teaches transformation.

A 2nd Degree Black Belt under the legendary Master Fabricio Moreira, Coach Doty has competed and won at the highest levels of sport — including the prestigious IBJJF European Championship and the World Pro Championship.

His military background adds a dimension few coaches can match. As Head Instructor to over five elite military units including special forces, Coach Doty built training methodologies for real-world scenarios — where composure, precision, and controlled aggression are essential. That same philosophy underpins every OMMA program.

As founder of Qatar MMA — the nation's largest BJJ & MMA institution — he left a lasting mark on combat sports culture across the Gulf. Now he's building the same legacy here in Oman.
